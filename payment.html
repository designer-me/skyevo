Issues with Payments - Pending Payments:

Payments can sometimes fail or remain in the pending stage. Some of the reasons could be:

  • Incorrect debit or credit card details are entered.
  • Card may be blocked or card details are no longer valid or need to be updated.

  • The connection between Skyevo and Your bank or issuer failed due to some technical reasons and the transaction could not be completed.

If You face any of the above payment issues, try placing the order again in a short while or contact Your bank or issuer for technical assistance.
