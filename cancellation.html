CANCELLATIONS AND RETURNS

Skyevo ensures to provide the excellent services you deserve. We have a perfect platform that allows you to buy the efficient resources of your needs. We have 100% return policy for the products purchased from www.skyevo.in & not used within 7 days of delivery.

For all your cancellations and return please feel free to contact our Customer Support team at support@skyevo.in without any hesitation. Unless a product is defective. The only condition for accepting any return is that the product should be unused and should be in the original condition like at the time of delivery.

The product to be returned / exchanged should be returned in its actual condition at the time of delivery. Please ensure that clothes and all other items are not used (other than trials), altered, washed, soiled or damaged. Retain original tags and accessories. Branded packaging should be returned in an undamaged condition with the product.

However, damages due to mishaps or misuse of product are not covered under the policy. Socks and clothing freebies are ineligible for returns. Products with tampered or missing serial numbers are likewise ineligible.

'Damage' or 'defect' is as defined under the manufacturer’s warranty. Please return the item in the actual condition you received it with all its original packaging and accessories.

Replacement for the product is possible on demand and subjected to availability of the same.

To initiate a return simply write to us at our Customer Support team at support@skyevo.in and we will take care of the rest. Our executives are available and pick the product from the place of delivery at free of cost.

How can I cancel my order?

This website works only on an online purchase model as well as with Cash On Delivery. If you wish to cancel an order, please contact our Customer Support team at support@skyevo.in and let us know the reason for it. Our Subject Matter Experts will review the reason for your cancellation request and try to resolve the objective if possible. If you still do not wish to continue with your order, we’ll cancel it without any further delay. Once the order is cancelled, a confirmation email will be sent to your registered email ID. The Particular order shall reflect as CANCELLED in your user panel.

I've still not received the refund in my bank account. Why?

If you have received a mail from us confirming your refund request, then rest assured that we have initiated your refund request and are following up with financial organisations for the same. You will be notified of the same. Sometimes financial organizations take a longer time to process the refund request. However, if the refund hasn't happened by the date we promised, you can contact us. We will gladly help you.

Refund Policy

Please allow up to 10 working days after receipt of your item to process any refunds. Where the original payment was made by credit / debit card we will refund any amount to the original card that was used. Where an order was paid for by cash on delivery, refunds will be made by bank transfer / NEFT.

Your money will be refunded to you within 7-10 business days of you making the cancellation request. (in any case of a refund we would only refund the money once we have received the product back at our /seller’s warehouse) Please get in touch with the banks directly in case of any delays post confirmation of cancellation/refund by Skyevo. If there be any delays caused at the end of Skyevo, we will update you of the same. Skyevo shall not be responsible for (a) any delays caused by the banks in making the refund; and (b) delay in processing the refund by Skyevo of not more than 15-20 days, In the above enumerated cases Skyevo’s liability shall be limited to the amount of refund.
