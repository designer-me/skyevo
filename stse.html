STSE -

SKY TALENT SEARCH EXAMINATION { STSE } is a program conducted by the organization to identify extraordinary talents in students to promote and encourage them to develop and improve their skills through cash rewards & many other assistance in their education. This scholarship exam can be taken by both school and college students.

It is a scientifically designed, skill-based assessment developed in India for Indian schools, after a detailed study of the syllabi. Rather than testing rote learning, it focuses on measuring how well skills and concepts underlying the school syllabus have been learnt by the student. The test provides information on the strengths and weaknesses of individual students and also entire classes.

For the conduction of SKY TALENT SEARCH EXAMINATION (STSE) the company is associated with KG SKY SAMAJ KALYAN SAMITI - a well known educational organization that conducts a number of educational activities all over the state. it is a professionally managed progressive organization in the field of education, established in the year 2016 by eminent personalities from various fields .{registered under the, 1973 act of Registry of Society in Madhya Pradesh - Reg. No. 11724}

All the process of this exam are completely online starting from registration to conduction of exam to declaration of result.

Students are provided with full support for the preparation through online Mock Test, Sample Paper & Online Video Lectures.
